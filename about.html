About Us

Vikas Kumar

Our Co-Founder is dedicated to improving lives by providing education and healthcare to underprivileged communities. He works tirelessly to create opportunities for a better future.

Welcome to My Foundation:

At My Foundation, we believe that everyone deserves access to education, healthcare, and dignity. We aim to empower marginalized communities and provide the support they need to thrive.

Our Vision:

A society where everyone has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth, regardless of background or financial situation.

Our Mission:

To provide educational materials, organize health camps, and promote health awareness among women and children. We strive to make a positive impact and help individuals reach their full potential.

Our Objectives:

  • Education: Support children with learning materials and education opportunities.
  • Healthcare: Organize health camps and provide essential medical care.
  • Empowerment: Promote awareness and resources to uplift communities.

Our Values:

  • Compassion: We care for the well-being of everyone we serve.
  • Integrity: We work transparently and honestly.
  • Empowerment: We enable communities to create better futures.

Join Us:

Together, we can create a brighter future for those in need. Join our mission and make a lasting impact.
